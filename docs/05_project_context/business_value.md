# Business Value and KPIs

FinDocAI is designed to deliver tangible value to Kifiya's Intelligent Data Decisioning (IDD) platform by driving efficiency, reducing costs, and mitigating risk.

## 10.1 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Efficiency and Productivity Gains

-   **Manual Review Time:** Reduce average document review time from **15 minutes to 2 minutes**, an **87% reduction**.
-   **KYC/Loan Processing Cycle:** Decrease the end-to-end time for processing a loan application or KYC check from **48 hours to 2 hours**, making the process **96% faster**.
-   **Human Error Rate:** Lower the error rate in data entry and verification from a human baseline of **5% to under 1%** with an AI-assisted workflow.

### Financial Impact

-   **Cost Per Application:** Drive down the operational cost per loan application from an estimated **$25 to $5**, achieving an **80% reduction**.
-   **Processing Throughput:** Increase the capacity of a single team to process applications from **100 per week to 500 per week**, a 5x improvement.
-   **Revenue Unlock:** Faster and more accurate decision-making can lead to higher customer satisfaction and increased approval rates for qualified applicants.

### Risk Mitigation

-   **Automated Fraud Detection:** Proactively identify potential fraud in documents like bank statements and invoices through pattern and anomaly detection.
-   **Improved Consistency:** Ensure that all documents are verified against the same set of rules, reducing the risk of human inconsistency and bias.
-   **Enhanced Compliance:** Provide a complete and immutable audit trail for every document, simplifying regulatory compliance and reporting.
